# Product Overview — Hello Orias

## Product Identity
- **Name**: Hello Orias
- **Type**: Simple personal landing page
- **Tagline**: A clean, one-section personal greeting page

## Purpose
A minimal single-section landing page whose sole purpose is to display a warm personal greeting: **"Hello Orias"**. No navigation, no forms, no footers — just one crisp section.

## Audience
Anyone who visits Orias's personal URL. The audience is broad and casual; the experience is instant and frictionless.

## Design Principles
- One section only — no scrolling, no multi-part layout
- Clean, minimal, whitespace-led
- Typography-first: the greeting is the hero

## Tone & Voice
Warm, personal, unpretentious, simple.

## Scope
Single page, single section, single message: **"Hello Orias"**.

## Strategic Principles
- Less is more — every element earns its place
- The greeting is the product
- Speed and simplicity over feature breadth
